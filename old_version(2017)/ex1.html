Multicollinearity in Decision tree

Decision trees make no assumptions on relationships between features. It just constructs splits on single features that improves classification, based on an impurity measure like Gini or entropy. If features A, B are heavily correlated, no /little information can be gained from splitting on B after having split on A. So it would typically get ignored in favor of C.

How to split: Greedy search. Regression MSE/MAE classification: Gini Impurity, information entropy.
